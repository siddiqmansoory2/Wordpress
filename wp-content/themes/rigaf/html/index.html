Law Firm Software for DUI Lawyers & Criminal Defense Attorneys

The ebb and flow of legal work is the double-edged sword faced by all growing firms. You want to grow, but more clients means more work, and without the right systems in place, it means more followup with clients, more phone calls, more time spent managing the day to day, and less time building the dream firm you imagined.

Straightforward insights

See your numbers in one glance. No guesswork. No interpretation. A built in scorecard for your firm. Day to day, week to week, month to month, get a clear picture of the health of your practice, so you can be the leader your firm needs.

Simple case management

Automate every step in a case and communicate progress to your clients at the same time. Know who does what and when and what needs done next. Each completed task or movement in a case creates a clear next step with a due date and someone to do it.

Killer intake process

Turn non-lawyers into salespeople by giving them a “paint by numbers” approach to picking up the phone and turning calls into clients. Exact scripts, strategy and training program included.

Seamless communication

Automate client updates with pre-written email and SMS templates that auto-magically blast off as your leads become clients, and your clients move through different stages of their case. Pre-built templates are already packaged within the software. Easily customized or ready to go. You’re in control.

Out of this world support

Software setup and training. Process review and implementation. Live coaching sessions and Q&A. Don’t get left behind. Get leveled up. Join our community of entrepreneurial attorneys who are building the firm they dreamed of.
